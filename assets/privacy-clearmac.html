Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Clear Mac helps you free up disk space, remove unnecessary files, and keep your Mac organized. Whether you are a developer, student, professional, or everyday Mac user, Clear Mac makes storage management simple.

Your Mac can accumulate gigabytes of caches, temporary files, logs, old downloads, application leftovers, and development data over time. Clear Mac helps you find and safely remove files you no longer need.

1. Features

  • Free up storage space by removing junk files
  • Clean system caches and temporary files
  • Remove application leftovers
  • Find large files taking up your disk
  • Manage developer files from Xcode, simulators, Android Studio, Flutter, and more
  • Keep your Mac organized and optimized

2. Privacy by Design

Built with privacy in mind. Your files stay on your Mac while you decide what to remove. Clear Mac does not upload your personal files, logs, or system data to any external servers.

3. Data Collection and Usage

Clear Mac operates locally on your machine. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data or the contents of your files.
